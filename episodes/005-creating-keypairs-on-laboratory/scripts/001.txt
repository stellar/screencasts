The Stellar Lab is an interactive toolkit for exploring the Stellar network.

When interacting with the Stellar Lab, you'll need to create a keypair to have a public and private key for your Stellar account.
<break time="2.0s" />
To create one, on the sidebar, go to Account, then Create Account Keypair.
<break time="1.0s" />
To randomly generate new credentials, click the generate button.
<break time="1.0s" />
You can save them to use later by clicking on save keypair.
<break time="2.0s" />
Now, you'll need to fund the account. In development, you can use the Friendbot tool. Just click on the fund button and your account will be created with 10 thousand lumens.
<break time="4.0s" />
To view all your saved keypairs, go to the "Saved" section on the sidebar.
